# 🤖 AGENTS Handbook: The "Zero to Hero" Simulation

This is not a manual—it is a **mission report**. We are simulating the "Best Case Scenario": a solo developer taking a raw idea and turning it into a SaaS using every single item in the **Portable Brainvibing** infrastructure.

---

## 🎬 Act I: The Big Bang (Setup & Deployment)

You have a blank screen and a dream. You start by "cloning" the intelligence.

1. **`scripts/deploy_foundation.py`**: You run this first. It’s the mechanical hand that reaches into the cloud and pulls down the `.agents` infrastructure into your empty folder.
2. **`.agents/DEPLOY_ME.md`**: You (or your AI) read this manual to ensure your environment—VS Code, Python, and the Antigravity IDE—is configured correctly for "Surgical Engineering."
3. **`scripts/verify_agents.py` & `scripts/audit_structure.py`**: You run these twins to confirm the bridge is stable. They check every file hash and link, ensuring the "Outer Brain" is perfectly connected.

---

## 🚀 Act II: The Connection (Initialization)

The brain is there, but it doesn't know you yet.

4. **`workflows/project-init.md`**: You trigger this workflow. It’s the handshake.
5. **`.agents/workspace_map.md`**: The system registers your new app here, mapping its location so it can be managed alongside your other projects.
6. **`.agents/catalog.json`**: The AI assistant scans this index to see which "Modular Personas" (Skills) it can summon for your specific task.

---

## 🏛️ Act III: The Architect's Vision (The Blueprint)

You explain your idea. The AI doesn't start coding; it starts **thinking**.

7. **`workflows/project-blueprint.md`**: You invoke this high-level ritual.
8. **`skills/project-architect/`**: The "Chief Architect" persona is summoned.
9. **`templates/BLUEPRINT.template.md`**: The architect fills this out, defining the "Brand Soul" and the user's emotional journey.
10. **`templates/ARCHITECTURE.template.md`**: The architect decides on the skeleton—choosing how the layers of your app will talk to each other.
11. **`templates/ROADMAP.template.md`**: You define the milestones. What is the MVP? What is V2.0?
12. **`templates/STYLE_GUIDE.template.md`**: You decide on the "Vibe"—the typography, the palette, and the liquid-glass aesthetics.
13. **`templates/SAAS_STARTUP_STRUCTURE.md`**: You define the business—how will you charge users? How will you scale?

---

## ⚖️ Act IV: The Law of the Land (Governance)

The AI builder is ready, but it must follow the "Golden Rules."

14. **`rules/common/context_hierarchy.md`**: The AI learns the priority—if you write a `local-` rule, it **must** ignore the global standards.
15. **`rules/common/model_tier_protocol.md`**: The system calculates costs. It will use "Flash" for routine code and "Pro" for the complex logic.
16. **`rules/common/context7_economical_usage.md`**: It learns to be "Token-Lean," reading only the code it needs to save you money.
17. **`rules/common/git-workflow.md`**: It learns how you like your commits—clean, descriptive, and atomic.
18. **`rules/common/security.md`**: It memorizes your safety gates—no API keys in the code, ever.
19. **`rules/common/agent_protocols.md`, `analytical_standards.md`, `connector_protocols.md`**: It learns how to communicate with other agents and how to analyze problems before acting.
20. **`rules/common/interaction_protocols.md`, `performance.md`, `reasoning_protocols.md`**: It masters the "Tone" and the "Logic" of your personal development style.
21. **`rules/flutter/dart-style.md` & `flutter_standards.md`**: It masters the stack, ensuring every widget is optimized for 60FPS.
22. **`rules/flutter/flutter_hybrid_architecture.md`**: It learns the specific way you mix Flutter with native or web elements.
23. **`rules/web/web_standards.md`**: If you build a web dashboard, it follows these rules for responsiveness.

---

## 🏗️ Act V: The Great Construction (Feature Build)

Now, the heavy lifting.

24. **`workflows/app-builder.md`**: You run this every day. It’s the engine that drives feature development.
25. **`skills/skill-db-expert/`**: The database specialist builds your Supabase tables and Row Level Security (RLS).
26. **`skills/skill-api-contract/`**: The safety specialist generates Zod/API schemas so bad data can't break the app.
27. **`skills/skill-data-logic/`**: The Flutter specialist generates your "Freezed" models and "Riverpod" state management.
28. **`skills/backend-architect/`**: The pattern expert implements the MVC or Repository layers.
29. **`skills/context-manager/`**: If you get lost in the code, this skill helps the AI find exactly where the bug is.
30. **`skills/skill-knowledge/`**: If you want to use a brand new library, this skill "ingests" the documentation in seconds.
31. **`canons/auth/canon.md`**: The AI builds the login screen exactly like your "Gold Standard"—no deviations.
32. **`canons/notifications/canon.md`**: It sets up the push alerts using your proven patterns.
33. **`canons/ui-patterns/canon.md`**: Every button and card follows your pre-approved design "Canons."
34. **`templates/canon.md.example` & `global-bridge.md.example`**: You use these to create new standards as your app grows.

---

## 🧪 Act VI: The Shield (Verification & Polish)

The code works, but is it "Surgical"?

35. **`workflows/strict-tdd.md`**: You turn this on for critical logic. The AI must write a failing test before it can write a single line of solution code.
36. **`skills/skill-qa-engineer/`**: The QA specialist runs multi-sequence user journeys, clicking every button to find edge cases.
37. **`skills/skill-ui-finish/`**: The Alchemist is summoned to add micro-interactions, gradients, and that "Liquid Glass" feel.
38. **`skills/ux-designer/`**: The designer simplifies the flow, ensuring the user never feels overwhelmed.
39. **`skills/flutter-debugger/` & `workflows/flutter-debug.md`**: When a weird bug appears, you use these to inspect the widget tree in real-time.

---

## 🛡️ Act VII: The Fortress (Optimization)

Ready for the world, but is it fast and secure?

40. **`skills/security-expert/`**: You run a final audit. The agent threat-models your APIs and storage.
41. **`skills/backend-optimizer/` & `skills/cache-optimizer/`**: They hunt for memory leaks and implement caching strategies to save database load.
42. **`skills/cost-optimizer/`**: The agent prunes your infrastructure, finding ways to save cents on every user request.
43. **`skills/eval-engineer/`**: You run "Prompt Regression" tests to ensure the AI's logic hasn't drifted over time.

---

## 🚢 Act VIII: The Launch (Deployment & Scaling)

The big day.

44. **`workflows/code-review.md`**: One final sweep of the code for "Surgical Integrity."
45. **`workflows/repo-squash.md`**: You clean up your git history, making it look professional for investors or collaborators.
46. **`workflows/prod-deploy.md` & `skills/release-manager/`**: The AI handles the CI/CD pipeline, pushing the app to the App Store and Cloud.
47. **`skills/tech-writer/`**: The agent generates your documentation, READMEs, and "How-to" guides for users.
48. **`templates/MEMORY.template.md` & `templates/SAAS_MEMORY.template.md`**: You record the session. What worked? What failed? The "Outer Brain" remembers.

---

## 📈 Act IX: The Evolution (Growth & Profit)

The app is live. Now you make it a business.

49. **`skills/skill-saas-growth/`**: You ask the agent for a TikTok marketing script or a Reddit engagement strategy.
50. **`templates/LEAN_TO_SAAS_MAPPING.md`**: You map your tech features to "Customer Benefits" for your landing page.
51. **`templates/startup_knowledge_base.md`**: You start building a library of industry-specific insights.
52. **`skills/agent-architect/`**: You decide to build a second AI assistant just to handle customer support.
53. **`skills/skill-agent-evolution/`**: You notice you've been correcting the AI's button styles. You say: *"Promote this style to a Canon."* The system evolves.

---

## 🛸 Act X: The Legacy (Maintenance & Relocation)

Years later, you want to move the project or clean it up.

55. **`skills/skill-project-relocator/` & `workflows/project-relocation.md`**: You move the entire project to a new directory. The "Outer Brain" doesn't break a single link.
56. **`workflows/safe-migration.md`**: You move your live production database to a new region without a second of downtime.
57. **`scripts/audit_repo.py`**: You run a final health check to ensure your legacy code still follows the rules you set at the beginning.
