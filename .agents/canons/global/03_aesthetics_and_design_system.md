# Aesthetics & Design System

The color system and visual aesthetics of Echo Scion are built to reflect the persona identity of User Eka Putra. The mandated colors are **Black, Dark Blue, Space Blue, Light Blue, White, Light Gold, and Gold**.

These colors are married with the "Biological Zen" concept and implemented systematically across all MainSystem applications (Stellaread, Luminote, Resonary, Vershine, Ekklesion, Nextra).

## Mapping Color Themes to MainSystem Design Tokens

In the `brand.md` documentation, persona color references have been translated into production-standard *Design Tokens*:

1.  **Black & Dark Blue / Space Blue** (Cosmic & Silence)
    *   **Resonant Navy (`#0B1220`)**: Serves as the primary *background* color. Represents outer space (*space blue* / dark silence), providing high contrast for light.
    *   **Ash Ink (`#1B2230`)** & **Warm Ash (`#2A3347`)**: Used for UI panels and cards, reinforcing dimensional depth (space/cosmic).

2.  **Light Gold & Gold** (Divine Light & Warmth)
    *   **Ethereal Amber (`#FFC76D`)**: Acts as the representation of gold/light gold elements. Used as the primary accent to indicate warmth, starlight, subtle success, or high-value items.

3.  **Light Blue & White** (Clarity & Purity)
    *   **Hush Blue (`#9FB6FF`)**: Represents *light blue*. Provides a cool visual touch, typically used to convey calming information.
    *   **Silver Dawn (`#C8D1E0`)** & **Bone White**: Used for primary text and very fine borders, radiating clarity amidst the dark background.

## Visual Aesthetics & Visual Identity

The color concepts above are merged into the interface design style with the following principles:

*   **Cosmic & Warm Light**: The entire MainSystem suite gives the impression of "warm light within dark space." It does not use a "dead" *OLED Black*, but rather a *Circadian Background* (Resonant Navy) that resembles the night sky.
*   **The Glass Signature (Glassmorphism)**: The application system uses *Glassmorphism* as a standard layer. It employs low transparency combined with very high blur (refracting the light behind it). This gives a sense of lightness, elegance, and *less friction*.
*   **Cosmic Visual Motifs**: Iconography and decorative elements use a fine-line approach: *concentric circles*, *wavelets*, and *small stars*, aligned with the "Resonance" and "Stellar/Luminate" themes.
*   **Anti-Dopamine & Calming Nature**: The combination of gold (amber), blue, and black is set to calm the nerves and biological systems (*Biological Zen*). There are no harsh neon colors or bright reds; everything is designed to feel like a mental sanctuary.
