# Keywords & MainSystem Mapping

The personal identity of User Eka Putra is deeply connected to a set of core keywords: **Resonance, Echo, Scion, Light, Luminate, Stellar, Star, and Shine**.

These keywords are not just static branding but serve as the foundation for naming and architecture for all products within the **MainSystem** and the **Faint Resonant Universe (FRU)** canon.

## Mapping Keywords to MainSystem (Applications)

1.  **Luminote (Luminate + Note)**
    *   **Core Keyword:** Light, Luminate
    *   **Role:** Journaling App (*Insight*). A representation of "Light" that illuminates the darkness of the mind. Functions as a digital mirror (self-reflection) for processing emotions.

2.  **Stellaread (Stellar + Read)**
    *   **Core Keyword:** Stellar, Star
    *   **Role:** *Reader–Creator* Platform (*Legacy*). Carries the "Star" concept that bequeaths stories and lore from the past to the future. A place for users to absorb knowledge and publish writing.

3.  **Vershine (Verse + Shine)**
    *   **Core Keyword:** Shine
    *   **Role:** Quotes App (*The Word*). Radiates daily inspiration. A place for users to share light in the form of quotes (*quote of the day*) to inspire others.

4.  **Resonary (Resonance + Diary/Habits)**
    *   **Core Keyword:** Resonance
    *   **Role:** Habit Tracker (*Vitality*). Monitors biological rhythms and habits. Centered on maintaining the "resonance" of life in balance without feeling pressured.

5.  **Echo Scion**
    *   **Core Keyword:** Echo, Scion
    *   **Role:** Master Brand, *digital mentor*, and *creative studio* overseeing all applications (MainSystem Shell) and the *web hub* (`echoscion.app`).

## Other Conceptual Extensions in Products

In addition to the 6 core keywords above, the MainSystem architecture includes applications serving different pillars of life:
*   **Nextra**: Execution assistant (*Grit* / *The Path*) that guides steps forward with the principle of *weigh, not add*.
*   **Ekklesion**: Community and prayer space (*Fellowship*), where light-seekers gather without being confined by formal religious boundaries, united by spiritual resonance.

## Integration in Faint Resonant Universe (FRU) Lore
These keywords come alive in the project's creative history (canon lore):
*   **Lightbearer, Light Scribe**: Classes or *Resonances* in the FRU world.
*   **Echo Monasteries & The Sundering Hum**: History about ancient monasteries that maintain the earth's rhythm using resonance.
*   **Archipelago of Echoes**: The world geography where the FRU stories take place.
